NeverLoseBill – Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

NeverLoseBill (“the App”) helps users securely store, manage, and analyze their bills with AI. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When signing in using Supabase Auth (email or Google), we collect your email address, display name (if provided), and authentication tokens.

2.2 Uploaded Files

When you upload invoices/bills, we store PDF/image files, extracted text (via AI/OCR), and metadata such as file name, size, and upload date.

2.3 Anonymous Device Information

We may collect OS version, region, crash logs, and performance data. We do not collect device identifiers, contacts, location, or similar sensitive data.

2.4 Anonymous Analytics

Usage duration, feature usage, and error reports may be collected anonymously.

3. How We Use Data

  • User authentication
  • Bill storage and retrieval
  • PDF/image text extraction
  • Improving app functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • User support

We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers.

4. How Data is Stored

All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL, Storage, Edge Functions). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL). Uploaded files are private by default.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect such data.

7. Data Retention

Data is stored as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

8. User Rights

  • Close your account
